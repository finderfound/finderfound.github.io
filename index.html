About finderfound

Why this exists and who it’s for.

finderfound is a small, opinionated portal for people who like dark themes, neon edges, and the overlap between nature, code, and cryptic art.

It’s not a marketplace, not a fund, not a brand—just a curated signal boost and a place to stash tools for building in public.
